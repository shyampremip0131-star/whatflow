Frequently Asked Questions

Is this the official WhatsApp Business API?
Yes! WhatFlow is an official Meta BSP using the official WhatsApp Business API. Your number gets green tick verification.
How long does setup take?
Most businesses are live within 30 minutes. Our guided onboarding walks you through everything step by step.
Are there per-message charges?
Meta charges conversation-based fees. Your plan includes a quota. We pass through Meta's rates transparently with no markup.
Can I use my existing number?
Yes, migrate your existing WhatsApp Business number to WhatFlow while preserving your verified name and green tick.
What's the delivery rate?
We consistently achieve 98%+ delivery rates. WhatsApp has one of the highest delivery rates of any messaging platform.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! Start with Starter plan free for 14 days — no credit card required. Growth and Enterprise also come with 14-day trials.
