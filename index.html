Is this the official WhatsApp Business API?
Yes! WhatFlow is an official Meta Business Solution Provider (BSP). Your number gets the green tick verification.
How long does setup take?
Most businesses are live within 30 minutes. Our guided onboarding walks you through everything step-by-step.
Are there per-message charges?
Meta charges conversation-based fees. Your WhatFlow plan includes a message quota, with transparent pass-through of Meta's rates.
Can I use my existing number?
Yes! Migrate your existing WhatsApp Business number. Migration preserves your verified name and green tick badge.
What's the delivery rate?
We consistently achieve 98%+ delivery rates — far higher than email or SMS, making WhatsApp the best marketing channel.
